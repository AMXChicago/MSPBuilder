# Product Requirements Document

## Product Summary
MSP/MSSP Launch OS is a multi-tenant SaaS platform for founders and operators who need to define, launch, and standardize a managed services or managed security services business.

The MVP focuses on helping a user move from intent to operating readiness:
- define the business model
- build a service catalog and package structure
- model pricing and margin targets
- choose a supporting vendor stack
- generate reusable templates and onboarding assets
- establish an operational and security baseline
- track leading KPIs after launch

## Problem Statement
Most first-time MSP/MSSP founders assemble their operating model through disconnected spreadsheets, vendor notes, generic templates, and improvised processes. That creates avoidable pricing mistakes, inconsistent delivery, weak service packaging, and technology choices that do not fit a multi-tenant services business.

## Target Users
- First-time MSP founders
- MSSP founders or consultants productizing security services
- Boutique IT consultancies moving into recurring revenue services
- Small operators standardizing delivery and improving margins

## Core Jobs To Be Done
1. Help me define a viable MSP/MSSP business model for the market I want to serve.
2. Help me package services and price them with realistic margin guardrails.
3. Help me select the core stack I need without relying on vendor hype.
4. Help me produce customer-facing and internal operating assets quickly.
5. Help me launch with enough operational structure to deliver consistently.

## MVP Capabilities
### Founder Profile
- capture founder experience, confidence, service motion, and target geography
- drive recommendations from operator context rather than static defaults

### Business Model
- define ICP, vertical focus, company size, delivery motion, and revenue strategy
- store target margin posture and packaging intent

### Service Catalog And Packaging
- define service definitions by category
- group them into packaged offers
- preserve package composition as a reusable business asset

### Pricing Engine
- store pricing strategies and margin targets
- compare package pricing against floor and target margins
- reserve complex dynamic pricing for later phases

### Stack Recommendation Engine
- maintain a vendor catalog
- evaluate vendors through centralized policies
- produce explainable recommendations tied to operating context

### Templates And Contracts
- catalog templates by type, version, and required variables
- support future rendering and approval workflows

### Onboarding And SOPs
- track onboarding checklists and ordered steps
- store SOP metadata and versioning

### Security Baseline
- maintain reusable baseline controls by category
- support operational guidance without becoming a GRC platform

### Marketing And Launch
- capture launch campaigns and offers
- keep GTM scaffolding close to packaging and ICP choices

### KPI Tracking
- record baseline metrics for margin, pipeline, client count, MRR, and onboarding speed

## Non-Goals
- company formation execution
- tax and legal advice
- full contract authoring workflow
- vendor API integrations
- invoice generation or billing automation

## Success Criteria For Foundation Phase
- domain boundaries are explicit
- normalized schema exists for MVP entities
- rules and templates have independent modules
- frontend and backend share contracts without duplication
- documentation is sufficient for the next implementation sprint
